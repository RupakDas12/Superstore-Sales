Superstore Sales Analysis
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail superstore dataset of 9,994 order records to uncover revenue trends, top-performing products, and regional insights — actionable for inventory planning and sales strategy.

Overview
DetailInfoDatasetSample Superstore (Kaggle)Records9,994 ordersPeriod4 yearsToolsPython, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Jupyter Notebook

Key Findings

West region is the top revenue generator (~$25K), outperforming all other regions
Technology is the highest-grossing category (~$36K), followed by Furniture and Office Supplies
November–December are consistent peak sales months across all 4 years — useful for seasonal inventory planning
Canon imageCLASS 2200 Copier leads product revenue at $1.6K — nearly 2.5x the second-highest SKU
